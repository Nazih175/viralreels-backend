Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime by opening the main App interface, navigating to Settings > Plan & Billing, and clicking the "Cancel Subscription" button. Your limits will remain active until the end of your billing month.

How can I permanently delete my data?

In compliance with global data privacy laws, you can securely wipe your ViralReels vault and safely purge your entire account metadata directly inside the app. Simply browse to Settings > Danger Zone, and hit "Delete Account".
